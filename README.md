# WhisperFin

Modular architecture of WhisperFin, the financial platform that sees market-disrupting trends before they happen.

Modules include:
- Platform Dashboard. The user-facing platform. Contains the outputs of subcomponents and final financial decisions.
- Trend Scorer. Takes in the social media data and calculates the aggregate likelihood of events.
- Twitter Radar. Crawls and processes relevant social media data.
- Stockdata Extraction. Auxiliary component to extract public stock data.
- Validation Datasets. Dataset of stock market crashes and booms.
